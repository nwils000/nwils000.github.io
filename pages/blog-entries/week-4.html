A New Chapter

Reflecting on my onboarding experience, I contemplate what was helpful while working remotely, what hindered my progress, and how I overcame those challenges.

What I Found Helpful Working Remotely

I found separating myself from distractions to be key when working remotely. It's especially important for me to give full attention to my projects. I managed this by working at the library or at night when it was quieter, allowing me to focus completely.

What Hindered My Progress

The biggest blocker during onboarding was not properly pseudo-coding. The eagerness to jump into coding meant I didn't spend enough time planning. This led to logic issues in my projects, like the alarm clock, which required extensive debugging that could have been avoided with more thorough pseudo-coding.

Overcoming Challenges

I overcame challenges by being very active on Slack, reaching out for help, and diving into resources about topics I was less familiar with. This proactive approach was crucial for navigating through the difficulties I faced.
