A New Chapter

What was helpful during onboarding working remotely? What hindered your progress during onboarding? How did you overcome challenges during onboarding?

What I Found Helpful Working Remotely

I found separating myself from distractions to be key when working remotely. Especially while working on a project, it's important for me to give it my full attention. I achieved this by working at the library or at night when everyone else is asleep.

What Hindered My Progress

The biggest blocker to my progress during onboarding was not properly pseudo-coding. I get too anxious to start coding that I skip spending adequate time planning it out beforehand. This led to logic issues, especially with my alarm clock project, that took me hours of debugging which could have been avoided by proper pseudo-coding.

Overcoming Challenges

I overcame challenges by being very active on Slack, reaching out for help, and diving into resources on topics I wasn't familiar with.
